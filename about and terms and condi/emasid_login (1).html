Terms of Use & Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the eMASID Community Portal, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Use. If you do not agree, please do not use our platform.

2. Use of the Platform

eMASID is a government-facilitated platform for reporting, tracking, and resolving community issues. You agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information when filing reports.
  • Use the platform solely for lawful, community-related purposes.
  • Not submit false, malicious, or defamatory reports.
  • Maintain the confidentiality of your login credentials.

3. Data Collection & Privacy

We collect personal information such as your name, email, and barangay affiliation. Your data is:

  • Stored securely and never sold to third parties.
  • Used exclusively to improve barangay-level community services.
  • Subject to applicable Philippine data privacy laws (Republic Act No. 10173).

4. Barangay Official Responsibilities

As a registered Barangay Official, you are accountable for the timely response to reports filed within your jurisdiction. Misuse of official access may result in account suspension.

5. Intellectual Property

All content and functionality of eMASID is protected by applicable intellectual property laws. Unauthorized reproduction is strictly prohibited.

6. Limitation of Liability

eMASID and its administrators shall not be held liable for any indirect or consequential damages arising from use of this platform.

7. Amendments

We reserve the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use constitutes acceptance of revised terms.

8. Contact

For questions, contact your local ICT office or email support@emasid.gov.ph.

About eMASID

eMASID is a digital community platform designed to bridge the gap between Filipino residents and their local barangay officials — making governance more transparent, responsive, and inclusive.

Our Mission

To empower every Filipino community by providing a simple, accessible, and trustworthy platform for reporting local issues — ensuring every concern is heard, acknowledged, and resolved.

Who Is This For?

  • Residents — Report road damage, flooding, waste issues, and more.
  • Barangay Officials — Manage, prioritize, and resolve concerns efficiently.
  • Local Government Units — Gain data-driven insights on community needs.

Technology & Privacy

Built with modern web technologies, eMASID is committed to protecting user data in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

Developed in partnership with local government units across the Philippines.
